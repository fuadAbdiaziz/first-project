ABOUT US

CC's Sports Store is an organisation which was founded in March 12th, 2023. It was founded to produce, distribute, and deliver quality sportsware products to the die-hard sports fans across the country.

We target customers who are in need of sportsware, from football to rugby, to tennis and swimming, we strive to satisfy the needs of all types of sports fans.

We make our exclusive products from natural material which is found locally, using high-level technology in our factory. We also distribute original sportsware from other top brands from across the world.

Reaching us has never been easier. Through our website, mobile application, and our mobile contacts, customers are able to reach us for enquiries, complaints, and deliveries.

At CC's, our prizes are customer friendly, so you don't have to worry about digging deep into your pockets. Valued customers also receive occasional discounts, so they can continue to enjoy our never ending services at a reduced cost.

We thank you in advance for shopping and interracting with us. We hope to improve our services to better suit your needs.
